About Us

In an age where all information is available at fingertips, planning a holiday still means visiting multiple portals, and comparing multiple suppliers to arrive at the best combination of itinerary-budget! And while we would love to decide where to go – we still want a concierge – the travel agent to help us plan the holiday.We realized that with travel portals of today designed in late 90s, there’s a lot left to be re-imagined in the holiday planning and booking space. Even today, it can take up to 72 hours for a travel agent to create a customized holiday based on the traveler preferences; we are fixing this!

Our flagship product, BarefootWanderers.com, is an online service for travellers to automatically create holidays personalised to their requirements and allows them to book all components at once within seconds.

mobile app, InTrip is a service provided as a part of BookMyTraveller.com to provide travellers access to vouchers for all services such as hotels, activities, transfers, intercity, cars, ferries, flights, rail passes and meals.
